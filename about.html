About

WELCOME TO LearnX

LearnX is a simple and interactive learning platform designed for software development students. Our platform provides easy access to study materials and enables students to contact teachers for resolving doubts. We aim to make learning structured, engaging, and organized. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, LearnX helps you grow in software development with well-structured study resources. LearnX also offers a seamless way for students to connect with teachers, ensuring that doubts are resolved quickly and efficiently. This feature helps students gain clarity on complex topics and continue learning without interruptions.
